ABOUT HEALTH UTOPIA

Health Utopia is a start-up that aims to breach the gap between people with health concerns and products or services.

With the aid of registered health providers, services can reach you wherever you are without hassle. Personal Consultation with a licensed doctor, either in the comfort of your home or an appointment at the hospital.

Registered and licensed pharmacies in partnership with Health Utopia, give you the health products you need, from drugs, to medical aids and equipment which can be delivered to your doorstep with the speed of light.

As the world evolves, technology makes things easier for everyone, including the health sector and semantics. Health Utopia makes it easier to find services on the map, finding hospitals, nearby pharmacies, state of the art laboratories and specialist clinics. We make use of a database collected to keep you on the right track of finding whatever health service you need at any point in time.

Emergency services such as ambulances that can be hired, to home or accident scenes when needed with urgency.
